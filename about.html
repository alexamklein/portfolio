More about me.

When I am not coding, you can find me practicing law. I obtained my JD and a BCL (Bachelor of Civil Law) from McGill University in 2021. Having recently passed the bar in Ontario, I am currently working at the Superior Court of Justice in Toronto. Download my resume below to find out more about my work experience.

While I am currently based out of Toronto, I am fluent in French as I grew up in Montreal where I lived until I moved to Ontario's capital to complete my undergraduate degree at the University of Toronto. I ended up meeting my partner in the 6ix, which is why I came back to this city after briefly returning to Montreal to attend law school.

In my free time, you can find me reading historical fiction and fantasy novels, on walks with my french bulldog and my sphinx cat, or travelling around the world with my partner and taking pictures as I go. Keep scrolling to see some pictures from my past trips!
